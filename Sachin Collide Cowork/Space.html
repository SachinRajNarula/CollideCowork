Coworking Space

Collide’s shared coworking space offers all of the comforts, features and tools you need to get the the most out of your work day:

  • Seating for over 60 Members
  • Private and collaborative work areas
  • Coffee/tea/water/snack station
  • Charging outlets throughout
  • Three 65” screens with the day’s top news
  • Private room for confidential calls/meetings
  • Business Centre with office supplies for us
  • Fully licensed bar for Members only

Board Room

Collide’s Innovation Room - sponsored by Gage Communications - is a professional meeting room available for groups up to 12:

  • Board table seating for up to 12 people
  • Breakout group seating for 4 people
  • 70” wall-mounted flat screen TV
  • Full device plugin/projection connections
  • 12’ wide floor-ceiling dry-erase whiteboard
  • Standalone coffee/tea/water station
  • Charging outlets throughout
  • Licensed room for post-meeting social time

Event Space

Collide’s event space is the best location for your next party or event! Located in the heart of downtown Sarnia you can host your guests in a modern, one-of-a-kind setting that will create an unforgettable experience.

  • Fully licensed venue with staffed and managed bar
  • Customizable bar menu for your specific event
  • Venue capacity of 220 people
  • 3 65” screens for multimedia presentations
  • Interior atrium with large amounts of natural light
  • Built-in stage for performances/speeches/head table
  • 2 adjacent parking lots for guest parking
  • Seating, PA/audio system, lighting options included in rental
